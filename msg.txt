feat: 3-state dynamic workspace layout

- Implemented a responsive 3-state layout system for Chat and Notes.
- Moved Action Toolbar buttons to the bottom of the Notes editor.
- Vertically aligned the Download Markdown and Prepare PDF buttons.

Landing Page Mode (Default): Upon reloading, you will ONLY see the Sidebar + Chat (The Notes section stays completely out of the way to give you maximum focus).
Split Workspace: When you click the initial ❮ side-arrow on the right, it slides open the Notes workspace, giving you a side-by-side view (Chat on the left, Notes on the right).
Full Width Notes: Once the Notes area is open, a second set of side-arrows appears between them. You can click ❯ to close the Notes back down, OR click the new ❮ arrow to collapse the chat and expand your Notes to completely take over the full width of the screen!
